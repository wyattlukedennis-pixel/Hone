Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 31, 2026

Overview

Hone ("we", "our", "the app") is a personal skill-tracking app. We respect your privacy and collect only what's necessary to make the app work.

What We Collect

  • Account info: Email address and password (or Apple ID) for authentication.
  • Clips: Video and photo clips you record are stored on your device and uploaded to our secure cloud storage so they survive app reinstalls.
  • Journey data: Journey titles, chapter progress, streaks, and settings you configure.
  • Device info: Basic device identifiers for crash reporting and app functionality.

What We Don't Collect

  • We don't sell your data to third parties.
  • We don't use your clips for advertising or training.
  • We don't track your location.
  • We don't share your content with other users unless you choose to.

Third-Party Services

  • RevenueCat: Manages in-app purchases. See their privacy policy.
  • Apple: Handles payment processing for in-app purchases.

Data Storage

Your clips and account data are stored on secure servers. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from the app's settings.

Your Rights

You can request deletion of your account and all data by using the "Delete Account" option in the app settings, or by contacting us at wyattlukedennis@gmail.com.

Contact

Questions? Email wyattlukedennis@gmail.com.
